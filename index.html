Profile
Global Expertise

Dr. Adeyemi Agbelusi is a seasoned mediator, arbitrator, and facilitator with over three decades of professional experience. As the Regional Mediator for West Africa at the World Bank Group, he continues to shape the landscape of alternative dispute resolution across the continent and actively contributes to international arbitration panels.

His practice is built on a foundation of unyielding integrity, absolute neutrality, and a commitment to helping parties discover amicable solutions to their most challenging deadlocks. From cross-border telecom disputes to complex corporate infrastructure issues, he brings a deeply analytical yet profoundly human approach to every engagement.
