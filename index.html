Who I Am

A Trusted Neutral
for High-Stakes Matters

Dr. Adeyemi Agbelusi is Principal Partner at Berean Associates and the Regional Mediator (West Africa) for the World Bank Group, where he has successfully facilitated the resolution of complex, high-value disputes across multiple sectors and jurisdictions.

He is an Independent Arbitrator, CEDR Accredited Mediator, and IMI Certified Mediator, with wide-ranging experience as mediator, arbitrator, and tribunal secretary in international proceedings. He is a Member of the ICC ADR Commission and ICC ADR Centre, contributing to global policy development and best practices in dispute resolution.

Dr. Agbelusi is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (UK), a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Mediators and Conciliators (Nigeria), and a Fellow of the Society of Advanced Legal Studies, University of London. He is active within the African Arbitration Association and other global professional networks.

Within the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (CIArb), he serves as Branch Secretary of the Nigeria Branch, contributes to the Schemes and Training Committees in the UK, and is an Approved Tutor, regularly engaged in training and mentoring dispute resolution practitioners. He is also the Immediate Past Chairman of the Lagos Branch of ICMC.

Recognised for his pragmatic, people-centred approach, Dr. Agbelusi blends deep legal expertise with commercial sensitivity — making him a sought-after neutral for matters where clarity, trust and durability of outcome matter most.
